ABOUT US

Arrow Power is a private limited company registered under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs of India, adhering to all prescribed norms and guidelines. Our Board of Directors consists of experienced professionals from diverse sectors within the energy and manufacturing industries.

Their expertise empowers us to design and produce products that comply with international quality standards, ensuring superior energy efficiency and reliability. This commitment to excellence has earned Arrow Power widespread acceptance in the global renewable energy market, reflecting our dedication to sustainability and innovation.

Arrow Power, established in 1999, started with a vision to bring clean energy solutions to everyone. Based in Bangalore, India, our headquarters and manufacturing facility serve as the foundation of our operations. Over the years, we’ve expanded our presence across India with regional offices to reach more customers effectively.

We specialize in delivering clean and sustainable energy solutions, including Solar Photovoltaic systems, Solar Thermal technology, and Small Wind Turbines. Our products are designed to meet the diverse needs of households, agricultural operations, and commercial businesses, helping them transition to reliable and eco-friendly energy sources.
